How We're Different

Farmstop was launched with the intention of providing people with alternative solutions to how we shop groceries. In the past decade, over 100,000 family farms in the U.S. have had to close - struggling to compete with large farms. We aim to build an equitable solution for local farms by connecting them directly with customers.
